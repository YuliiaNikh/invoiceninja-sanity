What to look for

Choosing the right host for Invoice Ninja

Not sure which host to pick? Here are the key things to check.

⚙️

PHP 8.1+ support

Invoice Ninja v5 requires PHP 8.1 or higher. Confirm your host supports this before purchasing.

🗃️

MySQL or MariaDB

A MySQL 5.7+ or MariaDB 10.3+ database is required. Most hosting plans include this.

🔒

SSL certificate

HTTPS is strongly recommended. Most hosts provide free Let's Encrypt SSL certificates.

🔄

Cron job access

Invoice Ninja requires cron job access for recurring invoices and automated reminders.

💾

At least 1 GB RAM

Shared hosting with 1 GB RAM works for small setups. VPS is recommended for teams or heavy usage.

📍

Data location

For GDPR compliance or data sovereignty requirements, choose a host with servers in your region.
